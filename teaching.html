Teaching Philosophy

My courses are designed to facilitate development of analytical skills and introduce students to alternate viewpoints within a respectful environment in which students are encouraged to challenge dominant views and unearth underlying assumptions. By providing exposure to different disciplines and viewpoints, rigorous analytical training, and by facilitating peer-to- peer learning, my courses will prepare the next generation of scientists, engineers, and decision-makers to design equitable and sustainable strategies to improve the outcomes of human communities and natural ecosystems.
